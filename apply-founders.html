Built for Aspiring Startup Founders

Vision

Columbia Build Lab is looking for founders that tell a story. We believe that every founder is a sales-(wo)man. It is your job as a founder to find and recruit the best people to be on your team. As a prospective invisible co-founder, we are interested in how you came up with the idea/problem and how you are going to solve it.

Imperfection

Alexander Pope once said, “To err is human; to forgive, divine.” Everyone makes mistakes and so do founders. Columbia Build Lab wants to partner with founders that admit to their mistakes and own them. We are here to help you make fewer errors and give constructive criticism. Only then, we can build the best version of your MVP.

Adventure

Founding a startup is equivalent to starting a long journey. Although most startups fail, if you end up creating one that is successful, it will take you around 7 to 9 years for your company to exit. You will have to overcome many hurdles and oftentimes pivot to increase your revenue, retention rate, LTV and other different metrics. Columbia Build Lab exists to make your adventure less painstaking and shorten your roadmap.
